Koobideh Kabob

Yellow onions, 1 1/2 Minced & strained
Ground Beef, 1 1/2 lbs (80-85% lean)
Ground Lamb, 1 lbs (80-85% lean)
Garlic, 3 cloves peeled and minced
Egg, 1
Salt, 1 tsp
Ground Sumac, 1 tsp
Ground Black Pepper, 1/2 tsp
Turmeric Powder, 1/2 tsp
Unsalted Butter, 1/4 cup melted
Rest
Form onto skewers
Grill & Brush with butter
