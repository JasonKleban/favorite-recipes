Rice Crispy Treats

Unsalted Butter, 1/2 cu
Marshmellows, 12 oz
Kosher Salt, 5 g
Vanilla Extract, 1 tsp
Rice Crispies Cereal, 6 cu
Rainbow Sprinkles
Melt & Stir butter past foaming until clarified with golden-to-medium-brown specks. Remove from heat
Soften marshmellows, add very low heat as needed
Fold gently to an even mix
Shape gently into a rectangle in, say, a greased pan. Decorate with sprinkles
