Spanish Rice

Oil (Avocado, Grapeseed or Vegetable), 1 tbsp
Long grain white rice, dry, 2 cu
Tomato paste, 4 oz
Combine
Cook rice in rolling boil until rice begins to float (10 minutes)
Strain
Rinse away starch and unabsorbed salt with cold tap water
Steam
covered on low heat until hot (5-10 minutes)
